# Enterprise Assurance and Due Diligence

## Product boundary
Enterprise products provide contracted aggregate Decision Intelligence. They never provide identifiable physician/employer data or raw Decision Episode histories. Release requires purpose authorization, privacy class check, aggregation eligibility, disclosure review, and audit record.

## Release controls
Default minimum cohort is 20 distinct eligible humans, with stricter country/contract rules where required. Suppress cells below threshold, complementary suppress related cells that permit subtraction, suppress unstable cohorts, and prohibit narrow intersections that enable singling out. Repeated-query controls, minimum time windows, rounding/noise where appropriate, contributor caps, and export watermarking reduce differencing and reconstruction risk. Thresholds are policy-configurable and independently tested.

## Buyer package
Security questionnaire covers architecture, IAM, encryption, logging, incident response, BCDR, SDLC, vendors, testing, and certifications. Vendor questionnaire covers subprocessors, data locations, DPAs, breach notice, retention, AI, accessibility, and insurance. Due-diligence package includes system/data-flow diagrams, pen-test summary, policies, control ownership, DR test evidence, data dictionary, methodology, verification coverage, bias/quality metrics, sample provenance, and contract terms. Buyer-specific evidence is access-controlled and current-dated.
