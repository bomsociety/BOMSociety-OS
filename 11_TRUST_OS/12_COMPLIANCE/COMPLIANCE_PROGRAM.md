# Compliance Program

BTOS is designed to support—not self-certify—GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA-aware handling, regional privacy requirements, and accessibility. The compliance program maintains control inventory, risk register, data maps, records of processing, vendor/subprocessor reviews, training, evidence collection, internal tests, management review, and independent assessment plans.

Privacy operations support notice, consent/preference management, access, correction, deletion, portability, objection/opt-out, identity verification, authorized-agent handling, and response deadlines. HIPAA-aware architecture segregates health-adjacent data, limits use/disclosure by contract and role, uses audit/encryption safeguards, and requires legal review before treating data as PHI or accepting covered-entity obligations. Accessibility targets WCAG 2.2 AA for supported experiences, with design review, automated/manual testing, and remediation tracking.

Regional transfer/residency requirements are expressed as policy and vendor controls. Compliance mappings identify evidence, owner, test frequency, exception, and remediation; legal interpretation remains counsel-owned.
