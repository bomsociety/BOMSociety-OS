# Acquisition and Exit Readiness

Maintain a continuously current virtual data room. Technical DD: architecture, service inventory, APIs, SLOs, SDLC, IP and dependency inventory. Security DD: policies, access reviews, scans, penetration tests, incidents, BCDR evidence, audit logs, certificates. Privacy DD: data map, retention/deletion evidence, rights metrics, transfers, DPAs, subprocessors. AI DD: models, datasets, evaluations, safety controls, human oversight, agent permissions. Legal DD: corporate/IP/contracts/disputes. Vendor DD: critical vendors, terms, concentration, continuity. Data DD: lineage, quality, consent/purpose, schemas, aggregation controls, sample/bias methodology.

Readiness is operational: named owner, dated evidence, gap/risk register, remediation plan, and reproducible system demonstration. No acquisition process may relax the Constitution's prohibition on identifiable physician/employer data sales or disclosure of raw Decision Episode histories.
