# External configuration checklist
- **Cloudflare:** configure HSTS, CSP/frame-ancestors, nosniff, referrer and permissions policies; WAF/rate limits; challenge provider; logs.
- **Ghost:** secure cookies, member email verification, backups and CSRF protections.
- **Providers:** obtain OAuth/OIDC, enterprise SSO, registry and email-verification credentials; do not treat social login as physician verification.
- **Operations:** monitoring, alerts, restore drills, penetration test, secret rotation and SBOM/provenance retention.
- **Founder/legal decision:** privacy notice, consent wording/retention, research basis, enterprise methodology, DPA/vendor review, SOC 2 scope. No certification is asserted.
