## Content quality gate

An episode must improve a physician's decision‚ÄĒnot teach because a topic exists. It must synthesize high-quality public knowledge into original physician-specific guidance, use 30 seconds for recognition, 2 minutes for understanding, and 5 minutes for execution, and end with KNOW, DO, and SHARE. Where appropriate, source concepts from authoritative public institutions, specialty guidance, healthcare economics, classic business literature, and primary regulatory sources without reproducing copyrighted work.

## Intelligence domain contract

All ten permanent domains are **STATUS: IN DEVELOPMENT**. Their detailed purpose, real buyer and buyer decision, business problem, enterprise value, feeding episodes, validation and aggregation requirements, privacy safeguards, current maturity, and future product are published on the Enterprise page. No domain is commercially available until its stated validation and aggregation requirements are met.

Global minimums: a documented taxonomy, consented anonymous signals, cohort suppression below a documented threshold, time-window sufficiency, and a methodology/limitations statement. Individual physician data is never sold; patient data, raw documents, and identifiable profiles are excluded.
