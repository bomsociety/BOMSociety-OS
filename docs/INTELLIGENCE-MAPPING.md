# Intelligence Platform Mapping

## Governing rule

Every Decision Episode maps **1:1 to exactly one Intelligence Platform**. The mapping is mandatory: no Decision Episode is created, published, or measured without one primary platform home. An Episode may inform editorial learning elsewhere, but it never feeds a second intelligence platform.

| Decision Episode | Intelligence Platform |
| --- | --- |
| Compensation | Compensation Intelligence™ |
| Employment Contracts | Contract Intelligence™ |
| Artificial Intelligence in Practice | AI Adoption Intelligence™ |
| Revenue Cycle | Revenue Cycle Intelligence™ |
| Practice Economics | Practice Economics Intelligence™ |
| Practice Ownership | Practice Ownership Intelligence™ |
| Leadership & Operations | Leadership & Operations Intelligence™ |
| Physician Wealth | Physician Wealth Intelligence™ |

## Content engine standard

Each Episode intentionally supports three physician needs:

1. **BIG PICTURE · 30 SEC** — recognition through one visual, insight, mistake, and action.
2. **BRIEF OVERVIEW · 2 MIN** — understanding through a framework, checklist, comparison, decision tree, and example.
3. **DEEP DIVE · 5 MIN** — execution through calculators, templates, downloads, scripts, questions, references, expert pearls, reflection, and outcome planning.

Episodes are original, physician-specific work. Research may be informed by credible public sources, but content is distilled, organized, illustrated, adapted, and rewritten—not copied, generic, or summarized.

## Intelligence product gate

Before any platform produces enterprise intelligence, it must declare its primary Business of Medicine domain, buyer, deliverable, physician value, Episode source, aggregation threshold, validation requirement, privacy safeguards, and development status. All outputs are aggregated, validated, transparent about limitations, and never expose individual physicians.
